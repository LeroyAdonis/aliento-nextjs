Section 02

The Problem

The Burden of Preventable Disease

South Africa faces a quadruple burden of disease, with non-communicable diseases (NCDs) accounting for the majority of deaths. Hypertension, diabetes, obesity, and cardiovascular disease are rampant — yet largely undiagnosed in working-age populations.

64% Of SA deaths from NCDs
4.2M Undiagnosed hypertensives
87% Retail staff with risk factors (Adonis PhD)

The Screening Gap

Despite the availability of effective screening tools, uptake remains critically low. Barriers include:

  • Access — Employees cannot easily attend clinics during work hours
  • Awareness — Many do not perceive themselves as at risk
  • Cost — Private screening is expensive and not always covered by medical aid
  • Follow-through — Even when screened, few receive structured support to change behaviour

The Cost to Employers

Poor employee health directly impacts the bottom line:

  • R42 billion+ in annual productivity loss from employee illness in South Africa
  • 15-20% of payroll lost to absenteeism and presenteeism (unplanned absence)
  • Healthy employees are 2.5x more productive and 40% less likely to leave

The gap is clear: Employers need a turnkey screening solution that drives real behaviour change, at a price that makes sense at scale.

Section 03

The Aliento Solution

Three-Pillar Approach

01 | Screen

Comprehensive on-site health screening conducted at your premises. Measures include blood pressure, blood glucose, cholesterol, BMI, waist circumference, and health risk assessment questionnaire.

02 | Intervene

Each employee receives a confidential personal health report with a patient-adapted behavioural lifestyle intervention plan — tailored to their specific results, readiness to change, and personal goals.

03 | Refer

Employees requiring further care are referred through a structured onward pathway to a partner pharmacy group and a multi-disciplinary team of healthcare professionals.
