## What Is Personalised Medicine?

Personalised medicine (also called precision medicine) is an approach that takes into account **your unique genetic makeup, lifestyle, and environment** when making healthcare decisions.

### How It's Different

Traditional medicine often follows a "one-size-fits-all" approach. Personalised medicine recognises that:

- Your genes influence how you respond to medications
- Your environment affects your disease risk differently
- Your lifestyle choices interact with your biology in unique ways

### The Benefits

- **More accurate diagnoses** through genetic testing and advanced diagnostics
- **Targeted treatments** with fewer side effects
- **Preventive care** tailored to your specific risk factors
- **Better outcomes** through personalised monitoring

### It's Not Just About Genetics

While genetics plays a role, personalised medicine also considers:

- Your family medical history
- Your environment and exposures
- Your lifestyle factors
- Your preferences and values
