## Living Well with Chronic Conditions

Managing a chronic condition — whether it's diabetes, hypertension, or another long-term illness — is a marathon, not a sprint. Here are five evidence-based tips to help you along the way.

### 1. Build a Routine You Can Stick To

Consistency is your best friend. Take medications at the same time daily, schedule regular check-ups, and build healthy habits into your existing routine.

### 2. Know Your Numbers

Track your key metrics — blood pressure, blood sugar, weight, whatever applies to your condition. **Knowledge is power**, and these numbers tell your story.

### 3. Communicate with Your Healthcare Team

Don't wait until your next appointment to mention something concerning. Keep a log of symptoms and questions. Your doctor can only help if they know what's going on.

### 4. Move Your Body

Regular physical activity — even just 20 minutes of walking — can significantly improve outcomes for most chronic conditions. Find something you enjoy and make it a habit.

### 5. Prioritise Mental Health

Living with a chronic condition takes an emotional toll. Don't neglect your mental wellbeing. Seek support, connect with others, and remember: **it's okay to ask for help**.
